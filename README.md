# Product-Page
A **landing page** for a product, created as part of the **Front-End Development course** at **Route Academy**.  
This page is designed to showcase a product online in an attractive way, highlighting its features and encouraging users to make a purchase.

1. **Hero Section**:  
   Eye-catching main section with product images and a strong call-to-action.

2. **Specifications Section**:  
   Compares the product with competitors and highlights why our product is better.

3. **Reviews Section**:  
   Displays customer reviews and ratings to build trust.

4. **Related Products Section**:  
   Shows other products related to the main product for cross-selling.

5. **Navbar**:  
   Navigation links to quickly move between different sections of the landing page.

6. **Footer**:  
   Includes essential information and links at the bottom of the page.
